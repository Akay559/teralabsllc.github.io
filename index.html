Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026  ·  Last reviewed: April 2026

Summary: TYM is designed so that your financial data is stored only on your device. Teralabs LLC does not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal or financial information. This policy explains exactly what data is involved, what isn't, and how Apple's services interact with TYM.

1. Who we are

Teralabs LLC is the developer of TYM — Track Your Money, available on the Apple App Store. References to "we," "us," or "Teralabs LLC" in this policy refer to Teralabs LLC as the developer and publisher of TYM.

2. Data we do not collect

By design, TYM does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following through Teralabs LLC-operated infrastructure:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information
  • Your financial transactions, budget amounts, or savings data
  • Your device location
  • Behavioural analytics or usage patterns
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Advertising identifiers

We do not operate any server, database, or backend service that receives data from TYM users.

3. Data stored on your device

All information you enter into TYM — including transactions, categories, budgets, payment methods, savings goals, and app settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or enable iCloud sync as described below.

4. Apple App Store analytics

Apple may provide Teralabs LLC with aggregate, anonymized download and crash statistics through App Store Connect. This data does not include any personally identifiable information and does not reveal who has downloaded or used TYM. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. iCloud sync (optional, Pro feature)

TYM Pro users may optionally enable iCloud sync. When enabled, your TYM data is synchronized through your personal Apple iCloud account. This sync occurs between your devices via Apple's infrastructure. Teralabs LLC has no access to data stored in or transmitted through iCloud. iCloud sync is governed by Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy. You may disable iCloud sync at any time through iPhone Settings → your name → iCloud → TYM.

6. No account or registration

TYM does not require or offer user account creation. No registration, login, or personal information is required to use the app. You are not identifiable to Teralabs LLC through your use of TYM.

7. No bank or financial institution connection

TYM does not connect to, interface with, or retrieve data from any bank, financial institution, or third-party financial data provider. All transactions are entered manually by you. Teralabs LLC has no access to your banking credentials, account numbers, or bank transaction history.

8. In-app purchases and subscriptions

TYM offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (TYM Pro) through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Teralabs LLC does not receive, process, store, or have access to your payment card details, billing address, or any payment information. All subscription management is performed through your Apple ID.

9. Notifications

Bill reminders and spending alerts are generated and delivered locally on your device using Apple's UserNotifications framework. These notifications are scheduled on-device and do not involve any external server communication. Teralabs LLC does not use remote push notification services for TYM's bill or budget alerts.

10. Third-party SDKs and services

TYM does not incorporate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection SDKs. There is no integration with services such as Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or equivalent data collection tools in TYM's codebase.

11. Children's privacy

TYM is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because TYM does not collect personal information from any users, it does not collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has entered personal information into TYM in a way we have not anticipated, please contact us and we will investigate.

12. Your data rights

Because Teralabs LLC does not hold your personal data, we are unable to retrieve, modify, or delete data on your behalf that is stored on your device. You may delete all TYM data at any time by removing the app from your device. If you have enabled iCloud sync, you may delete synced data through your iCloud account settings. For questions about data Apple holds on your behalf, please contact Apple directly.

13. Data security

TYM relies on Apple's device security model, including device encryption, to protect locally stored data. Teralabs LLC does not independently operate servers that could be compromised to expose TYM user data. However, no technology can guarantee absolute security, and Teralabs LLC makes no warranty regarding the security of Apple's infrastructure or your personal device.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last reviewed" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will note them in TYM's App Store release notes. Continued use of TYM after updates to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.

15. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@teralabsllc.com. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.

16. Governing law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws applicable to Teralabs LLC in the jurisdiction in which it is registered. To the extent permitted by applicable law, any disputes arising under this policy shall be resolved in accordance with those governing laws.

How do I restore my Pro subscription after reinstalling TYM?

Open TYM → Settings tab → tap the "Upgrade to Pro" card → scroll to the bottom → tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If the issue persists, contact Apple Support as they manage all billing.

Will I lose my data if I delete TYM?

If you have not enabled iCloud sync, deleting TYM will remove your locally stored data permanently. We recommend enabling iCloud Backup on your iPhone (Settings → your name → iCloud → iCloud Backup) as a precaution. Pro users with iCloud sync active can restore data by reinstalling TYM on the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my TYM Pro subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → TYM Pro → Cancel Subscription. You retain Pro access until the end of the current billing period. Teralabs LLC does not handle refunds directly — for billing inquiries please contact Apple Support.

My transactions aren't appearing. What should I check?

Check the date filter at the top of the Transactions tab — it may be set to a period that excludes recent entries. If the issue continues, email us with your iPhone model and iOS version and we will investigate.

Can I move my data from another budgeting app to TYM?

CSV import is planned for a future Pro update (marked "Coming soon" in the pricing section). Until then, transactions can be entered manually. Most banks allow you to download a statement from their website if you need a reference.

Does TYM work on iPad?

Yes. TYM is designed for both iPhone and iPad. The iPad version uses a sidebar layout to make better use of the larger screen.

I have a feature request or feedback. How do I share it?

Email support@teralabsllc.com — we read every message. User feedback informs how we prioritise future development.
