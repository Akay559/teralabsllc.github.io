Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026 · Last reviewed: April 2026

Summary: TYM is designed so that your financial data is stored only on your device. Tera Labs LLC does not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal or financial information.

1. Who we are

Tera Labs LLC is the developer of TYM — Track Your Money, available on the Apple App Store. References to "we," "us," or "Tera Labs LLC" in this policy refer to Tera Labs LLC as the developer and publisher of TYM.

2. Data we do not collect

By design, TYM does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following through Tera Labs LLC-operated infrastructure:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information
  • Your financial transactions, budget amounts, or savings data
  • Your device location
  • Behavioural analytics or usage patterns
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Advertising identifiers

We do not operate any server, database, or backend service that receives data from TYM users.

3. Data stored on your device

All information you enter into TYM — including transactions, categories, budgets, payment methods, savings goals, and app settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or enable iCloud sync.

4. Apple App Store analytics

Apple may provide Tera Labs LLC with aggregate, anonymized download and crash statistics through App Store Connect. This data does not include any personally identifiable information and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. iCloud sync (optional)

TYM users may optionally enable iCloud sync. When enabled, your TYM data is synchronized through your personal Apple iCloud account via Apple's infrastructure. Tera Labs LLC has no access to data stored in or transmitted through iCloud. You may disable iCloud sync at any time through your device settings.

6. No account or registration

TYM does not require or offer user account creation. No registration, login, or personal information is required to use the app.

7. No bank or financial institution connection

TYM does not connect to or retrieve data from any bank, financial institution, or third-party financial data provider. All transactions are entered manually by the user.

8. In-app purchases

TYM offers optional subscriptions through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Tera Labs LLC does not receive or store your payment information.

9. Third-party SDKs

TYM does not incorporate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection SDKs.

10. Children's privacy

TYM is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because TYM does not collect personal information from any users, it does not collect personal information from children.

11. Data security

TYM relies on Apple's device security model, including device encryption, to protect locally stored data. Tera Labs LLC does not operate servers that could be compromised to expose user data.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last reviewed" date above. Material changes will be noted in TYM's App Store release notes.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at akay@teralabsllc.com.

How do I restore my Pro subscription after reinstalling TYM?

Open TYM → Settings → tap "Upgrade to Pro" → scroll to the bottom → tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Will I lose my data if I delete TYM?

If you have not enabled iCloud sync, deleting TYM will remove your locally stored data permanently. We recommend enabling iCloud Backup as a precaution.

How do I cancel my TYM Pro subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → TYM Pro → Cancel Subscription. You retain Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

Does TYM work on iPad?

Yes. TYM is designed for both iPhone and iPad. The iPad version uses a sidebar layout for the larger screen.
