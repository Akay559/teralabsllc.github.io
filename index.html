TYM — Track Your Money

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: TYM collects nothing. Your transactions, budgets, and savings goals are stored only on your iPhone using Apple's on-device database. Teralabs LLC has no servers that hold your data and no ability to see your financial information — ever.

What we collect

Nothing. TYM does not collect personal information, financial data, usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral data. We have no ability to see anything you enter into TYM.

Data storage

All data you enter into TYM — transactions, budgets, categories, savings goals, payment methods, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to export it yourself.

iCloud Sync (Pro feature)

TYM Pro users may enable iCloud sync. When enabled, your data is synced through your personal Apple iCloud account, governed entirely by Apple's Privacy Policy. Teralabs LLC has zero access to your iCloud data at any point. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iPhone Settings → your name → iCloud.

No account required

TYM does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information. You are completely anonymous to us. We do not even know you downloaded the app.

No bank connection

TYM does not connect to banks, financial institutions, or third-party financial data services. We have no access to banking credentials, account numbers, or transaction history from your bank. All transactions in TYM are entered manually by you.

In-app purchases & subscriptions

TYM Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Teralabs LLC does not handle, process, or store any payment information. All billing is managed through your Apple ID.

Notifications

Bill reminders and spending alerts are generated locally on your device using Apple's UserNotifications framework. Teralabs LLC does not operate push notification servers. No data leaves your device to deliver a notification.

Analytics & tracking

TYM contains no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, tracking pixels, or third-party monitoring services. There is no Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or equivalent in TYM's codebase.

Children's privacy

TYM does not collect information from any user, including children under 13. Because we collect nothing, there is no children's data to protect — but we are committed to this standard regardless.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will change the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be noted in the app's App Store release notes. Continued use of TYM after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

How do I restore my Pro subscription after reinstalling?

Open TYM → Settings tab → tap the "Upgrade to Pro" card at the top → scroll to the bottom of the paywall screen → tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription will restore automatically as long as you're signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

If iCloud sync is disabled and you delete TYM, your data will be lost. We strongly recommend enabling iCloud Backup on your iPhone (iPhone Settings → your name → iCloud → iCloud Backup). Pro users with iCloud sync enabled can restore all data by reinstalling TYM and signing in to the same iCloud account.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → find TYM Pro → tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period. Per Apple's policy, partial-period refunds are not available from Teralabs LLC directly — please contact Apple Support for refund requests.

My transactions aren't showing up. What do I do?

Check the date filter at the top of the Transactions tab — it may be set to a period that doesn't include recent entries. If the issue persists after adjusting the filter, email us with your iPhone model and iOS version and we'll resolve it quickly.

Can I import transactions from another budgeting app?

TYM Pro includes CSV import — you can import data exported from most other budget apps or downloaded from your bank's website. Free users can add transactions manually. Most banks allow you to download a CSV of your statement from their website.

Does TYM work on iPad?

Yes — TYM runs natively on both iPhone and iPad. The iPad version uses a sidebar navigation layout optimized for larger screens.

I have feedback or a feature request. How do I share it?

Email us at support@teralabsllc.com — we read every message. Feature requests from real users directly shape our roadmap.
